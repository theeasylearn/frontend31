Delicious Dhokla Recipe

Learn how to make this delightful Indian snack at home!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon eno fruit salt
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, ginger paste, green chili, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Mix the ingredients well to form a smooth batter. Ensure there are no lumps.
  3. Let the batter rest for 15-20 minutes to allow fermentation.
  4. Grease a dhokla steamer or a plate that fits into a steamer.
  5. Add eno fruit salt to the batter and mix gently. The batter will become frothy.
  6. Pour the batter into the greased plate and steam for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Once done, let it cool for a few minutes, then cut into squares or desired shapes.

Enjoy your homemade Dhokla! Serve it with mint chutney or tamarind sauce for an extra burst of flavor.

Delicious Fafda Recipe

Learn how to make this crispy Indian snack at home!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to make the dough)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, asafoetida, sesame seeds, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a smooth, lump-free dough with a thick consistency.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  4. Take a portion of the dough and using your hands or a press, shape it into thin, long strips (Fafda).
  5. Gently slide the Fafda strips into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove from oil and place on absorbent paper to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve Fafda hot with green chutney or sweet tamarind sauce.

Enjoy your homemade Fafda! Perfect for tea-time snacks or festive occasions.

Masala Dosa Recipe

Experience the South Indian delight with this spicy and crispy masala dosa!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 1 cup spiced potato filling
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Chutney and sambar for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat a dosa pan, spread dosa batter thinly, and drizzle with oil or ghee.
  2. Place spiced potato filling on one side, fold, and cook until crispy.
  3. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Enjoy the spicy goodness of Masala Dosa for breakfast or brunch!

Paneer Tikka Recipe

Indulge in the spicy and flavorful delight of homemade Paneer Tikka!

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into rings
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Mix yogurt, tikka masala, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice to make the marinade.
  2. Marinate paneer, bell pepper, and onion in the mixture for at least 1 hour.
  3. Thread marinated ingredients onto skewers and grill until charred.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Relish the spiciness of homemade Paneer Tikka – a perfect appetizer!

Aloo Gobi Recipe

Enjoy the perfect blend of potatoes and cauliflower in this spicy Indian dish!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 large potato, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add potatoes, cauliflower, and salt; cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.

Enjoy the spiciness of Aloo Gobi – a comforting vegetarian delight!

Baingan Bharta Recipe

Experience the smoky and spicy flavors of this roasted eggplant dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast the eggplant over an open flame until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft.
  2. Peel the roasted eggplant, mash the flesh, and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add mashed eggplant, salt, and cook until well combined and heated through.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or roti.

Enjoy the rich and smoky flavors of Baingan Bharta!
