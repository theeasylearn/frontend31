About us

Welcome to Patel Interior, your premier destination for all your furniture and home decor needs. Established in 1970 and nestled in the heart of Bhavnagar, our showroom boasts an extensive inventory of top-quality products. From bedroom sets, sofa sets, dining sets, chairs, TV units, and center tables to office furniture and a diverse range of home decor items and art, we have everything you seek under one roof. Experience the timeless elegance and exceptional craftsmanship that Patel Interior has been delivering for over five decades.
